Welcome to League for Life

Our Mission

The League for Life is an organization to defend civil rights and fight religious persecution for all people. We encourage and promote tolerance for all religions and creeds and endorse respect for the dignity of all people in any and every stage of human life.

Our Work

The Life League is a non-profit private organization which is available to all people regardless of racial and religious background. We have a number of centers throughout the nation to assist with court cases and other disputes involving civil and religious persecution. Your case will be always be considered privately and without any charge to you. You can apply for court funding and other financial support based on your situation. You can rally support for upholding religious freedom in your community through fundraisers and peaceful demonstrations. Our community of staff members and volunteers are available 24/7 for any of your needs.
